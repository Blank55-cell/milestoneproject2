Healing Calculator

Estimate how long it takes your units to recover.

Useful to know:

  • Units only heal while standing still in your own provinces
  • Core provinces heal faster than conquered ones
  • No healing during movement or combat
  • Mixed-HP stacks heal at the pace of the lowest units

Combat Modifier Chart

A quick look at what actually affects combat strength.

  • Fortress Bonus: +50% to +150% defense depending on level
  • Entrenchment: Units dig in over time and defend better
  • Morale: High morale boosts stats, low morale weakens them
  • Stacking Penalty: Huge stacks lose efficiency
  • Range Advantage: Artillery and railguns deal damage safely
  • Fire Control: Controls when and how your units engage

Extra notes:

  • Fortresses only help on defense
  • Moving resets entrenchment completely
  • Morale below 50% hits harder than most players expect
  • Artillery works best with infantry screens in front
  • Fire Control helps avoid unwanted fights

Information based on official Supremacy 1914 mechanics © Bytro Labs GmbH.

Unit Stats Reference

Quick stats for the most common units.

  • Infantry: 1.0 attack / 1.0 defense
  • Cavalry: 2.0 attack / 1.5 defense
  • Artillery: 1.5 attack / 1.0 defense (ranged)
  • Armored Car: 3.0 attack / 3.0 defense

Tips:

  • Infantry are cheap and reliable for holding lines
  • Cavalry are great earl-game for grabbing territory fast
  • Artillery wins fights without taking hits if positioned right
  • Armored Cars are solid defenders for important provinces
